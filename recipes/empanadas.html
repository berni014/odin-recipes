Empanadas


Description

Empanadas are savory pastries filled with a variety of ingredients such as beef, chicken, ham and cheese, or vegetables. They are made with a flaky dough that is folded over the filling and sealed, then baked or fried until golden. Crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside, these hand-held delights are perfect as appetizers, snacks, or even a full meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 empanada dough discs (store-bought or homemade)
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion,chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Olive oil (for cooking)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Filling: Sauté the onion and bell pepper in olive oil. Add ground beef, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook until the meat is browned. Let it cool, then mix in the chopped hard-boiled eggs.
  2. Fill the Empanadas: Place a spoonful of the filling on each dough disc. Fold the dough over and seal the edges by pressing with a fork or crimping by hand.
  3. Bake or Fry: Arrange on a baking sheet and brush with egg wash, then bake at 400°F (200°C) for 20-25 minutes, or fry in hot oil until golden.
  4. Serve: Let them cool slightly before serving. Enjoy warm!
